How to Use the Habit Tracker

Track Your Daily Habits: Log your progress each day to build and maintain good habits.

Select a Month: Choose the month you want to track. This will display a calendar.

Add Your Habits: Add up to 10 habits. Click “+” to add a new habit and name it by editing the header.

Log Your Progress: Click the cell under each habit to mark it as completed. A checkmark will appear.

Review Your Progress: View charts to see your progress over time and identify habits that need focus.

Customize Your Habits: Add or remove habits as needed using the “+” and “-” buttons.

Save Your Data: Regularly update your data to review your progress anytime.

Stay Consistent: Use the Habit Tracker daily to stay accountable and on track.
