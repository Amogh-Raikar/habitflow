How to Use the Habit Tracker

  • Select the month: Use the dropdown to choose the month you want to track.
  • Add/Edit habits: Click on the habit headers to edit or add new habits.
  • Track your habits: Click on the cells corresponding to the days to mark the completion of habits.
  • View your progress: Check the chart to see how well you are doing and review the completion statistics.
